CONCRETO

I do make concrete objects two hours a day.

Well I've got a deep passion for plants and I've got a deep passion for concrete too.. This made me start making things two hours a day, making, literally. Every day I dedicate myself to a sort of meditation.

I first started to experiment with concrete, trying to figure out which is the best way to mix water, sand and cement in order to create strong and cool objects made of concrete. I then started potting, and well, this is fun!! Hope you enjoy it as much as I do.
