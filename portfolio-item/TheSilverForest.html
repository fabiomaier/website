The Silver Forest

This project was based on the concept to build a contemporary hut real model with the following characteristics: no foundations, temporary, transferrable, reversable, incomplete, transformable and contaminable.

With all these characteristics you can watch the project from di erent environments such as, protective, safe, relaxation and simply just walk through and admire. The silver forest represents the possiblitiy to escape modern society, a woodlend corner of immaculate birches full of harmony in which anyone could stop inside.

The interior spaces are open inside but covered over head, simiular to a forest. The models base has been made of polystyrene 60x60cm covered with Das coloured brown. For the birches I cut and used real branches for a more realistic look.
